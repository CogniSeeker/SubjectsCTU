What this site is about

This site is for the subject “How to do (almost) anything” (“Jak vyrobit (téměř) cokoli” in Czech), which I am currently studying. Every week I learn something new and create a new project on a given topic, which I publish in the “Weeks” tab.

Topics included:

  1. Week 1: Creating a website and introducing the final project idea
  2. Week 2: 2D Laser cutting and stickers
  3. Week 3: 3D printing and scanning
  4. Week 4: Kinetic structures
  5. Week 5: Electronics manufacturing
  6. Week 6: Sensors and input devices
  7. Week 7: Actuators and output devices
  8. Week 8: Large machining
  9. Week 9: Wireless connection
  10. Week 10: Interfacing with computer, application programming
  11. Week 11: Moulding and casting
  12. Week 12-13: Computer-controlled machine
  13. Week 14-17: Final project

Also, at the end of the semester, we have to do a final project using the knowledge we have gained. You can find it on the Final project page.
