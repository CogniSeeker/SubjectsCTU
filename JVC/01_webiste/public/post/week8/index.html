Milling a Saloon sign using a CNC

An American Saloon sign, milled on a CNC

Idea

This week we had the opportunity to use the CNC milling machine, so I took this chance to capture the famous meme about the US - “WHAT THE F*CK IS A KILOMETER” (eagle screech)

on a wooden Saloon sign like this

Model design

Here is the model I designed in Fusion360

I decided to use all the wood available to me, so the dimensions of the sign are 50 x 30 cm (or 5 x 3 standart cheeseburgers).

When you design your model for a CNC machining, you need also to go to the Manufacture section, choose appropriate milling drill, its trajectory and boundaries of milling.

Note: you should choose the rign font with letter big enough for the milling drill to mill inside.
